Foundation Pro Mente Sana

In 2024 I completed the ensa Mental Health First Aid course by the Foundation Pro Mente Sana. Looking back, it was probably one of the most useful and meaningful things I have done for both my personal and professional life.

In 2026 I returned for the refresher course, which gave me the opportunity to revisit and strengthen the knowledge and tools needed to support people experiencing mental health challenges or crises until professional help can take over.

The course focuses on five key steps that help provide support in difficult situations, while also encouraging empathy, open conversations, and understanding around mental health. These are skills I believe are incredibly valuable not only in our personal lives, but also in professional environments and team settings.

Completing this refresher was a meaningful reminder that mental health awareness and support are not “nice to have” skills, but essential human skills.

I firmly believe that the ability to recognise when someone might be struggling and know how to approach and support them can make a real difference.
